Questions

Where Connected Carriers fits in your workflow

What do I actually send you?
The carrier's MC number and their phone number. That's it. If you have their email too, even better — we'll reach them both ways. You can also forward a rate con, carrier packet, or email thread. We work with whatever you have.
Do I need to replace my TMS?
No. This works alongside everything you already use. We're not a TMS, not a load board, not a compliance platform. We just verify the carrier is dispatch-ready before you assign the load.
Do carriers need to install anything?
No. They respond to a text message — either by clicking a link or texting back photos of their CDL, insurance, and cab card. No apps, no logins. They can do it from behind the wheel.
What if the carrier doesn't respond?
That's a signal too. We send them two messages. If they don't respond within 60 minutes, you get a DO NOT USE result. A carrier who won't verify before dispatch is telling you something.
What exactly do you verify?
FMCSA authority and operating status. Insurance — including OCR to check if the policy is actually current. CDL — driver name, expiration, class. VIN — extracted from the cab card and cross-referenced against VINs on the insurance policy. If the truck they're sending isn't on their insurance, you'll know.
Do you contact the carrier directly?
Yes — via automated text message. We request their verification documents on your behalf. We don't negotiate, don't discuss rates, and don't participate in the transaction. We just collect and verify.
Is this a subscription?
No. $9.99 per verification. Use it once a week or ten times a day — you only pay when you use it. No monthly minimums, no annual commitments.
What if I verify the same carrier again later?
Their profile is on file from their last verification. The system checks if anything has expired or changed since then. If everything is still current, you get a faster result.
