Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to replace my TMS?
No. Connected Carriers sits on top of your current workflow. Keep using Port, McLeod, or whatever you run today.
Do carriers need to install anything?
No. Carriers and drivers use simple links and texts. Nothing to download.
What happens after the free trial?
You can continue for $99/month or $999/year. If you don't want to continue, you don't get charged.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes.
Is this a full compliance platform?
No. Connected Carriers is a lightweight broker workflow layer that helps you filter carriers, chase missing info, and catch changes at pickup. It is not a compliance management system.
