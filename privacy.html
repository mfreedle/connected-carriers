Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Connected Carriers respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you visit our website, submit forms, upload documents, or use the Connected Carriers platform.

1. Information we collect

We may collect:

  • name, company name, phone number, email address, and business contact details
  • MC number, DOT number, and related carrier qualification information
  • broker interest and carrier interest form submissions
  • uploaded documents such as certificates of insurance, W-9s, carrier agreements, and related compliance materials
  • account login and session information
  • activity and workflow data related to setup packets, carrier review, and dispatch packets
  • technical information such as browser type, IP address, and basic usage logs

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • review broker and carrier submissions
  • facilitate carrier qualification and compliance workflows
  • manage setup packets and dispatch clearance workflows
  • communicate with brokers and carriers
  • improve the platform and support operations
  • maintain security, prevent misuse, and investigate issues

3. Document handling

Documents submitted through Connected Carriers may include sensitive business and compliance information. We use those documents only for operational, compliance, and workflow purposes related to the platform.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only:

  • with service providers that help operate the platform
  • when required by law
  • when necessary to protect the platform, users, or legal rights
  • with the broker or carrier involved in the relevant workflow, where operationally necessary

5. Data storage and security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information submitted through the platform. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we take steps to limit access and protect stored data.

6. Cookies and session data

Connected Carriers may use cookies and session technologies to support login, security, and normal site functionality.

7. Third-party services

Connected Carriers may rely on third-party infrastructure and verification services to operate portions of the platform. Those services may process information as needed to support platform functionality.

8. Your choices

If you want to update submitted information or have questions about your data, contact us using the information on our Contact page.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: admin@connectedcarriers.org
