Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Connected Carriers ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by HoneXAI. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is stored.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly when using the platform:

  • Broker accounts: company name, contact name, email, phone, and business details submitted during access requests and account setup.
  • Carrier submissions: MC number, DOT number, company name, contact information, equipment types, lanes, and any other details submitted through intake or interest forms.
  • Compliance documents: Certificates of Insurance, W-9 forms, and signed carrier agreements uploaded through the carrier setup packet flow. These are stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage.
  • Dispatch data: Driver names, phone numbers, VIN numbers, truck and trailer information submitted as part of per-load dispatch packets.
  • Session data: We use secure, HTTP-only session cookies to maintain broker login sessions. Sessions expire after 7 days.

How we use your data

  • To operate the carrier qualification and dispatch clearance platform
  • To verify carrier authority and compliance information via the FMCSA SAFER public API
  • To allow brokers to review, verify, and manage carrier compliance documents
  • To maintain audit trails of carrier decisions and dispatch activity

How data is stored

  • Platform data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway.
  • Compliance documents are stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage with a private access policy. Files are not publicly accessible. Broker access is provided through short-lived signed URLs.
  • Session data is stored server-side in the database and referenced via a secure cookie.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Carrier data may be shared with the broker who initiated the qualification or dispatch request. FMCSA authority data is retrieved from the public FMCSA SAFER API.

Data retention

We retain broker and carrier data for as long as the account is active or as needed to operate the platform. Compliance documents are retained for the duration of the carrier relationship. You may contact us to request deletion of your data.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to maintain broker login state. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.
