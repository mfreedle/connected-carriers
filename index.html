The process

Three steps to the network

No phone calls. No faxes. No waiting a week to hear back.

1
Submit your profile

Fill out your carrier qualification profile once — MC number, DOT, insurance certificates, equipment, and operating history. Takes about 10 minutes.

2
Get verified

The system checks your credentials against FMCSA in real time. Insurance is verified. Authority is confirmed. Your tier is assigned automatically.

3
Access the network

You're in. Brokers in the Connected Carriers network can see your verified profile. No more starting from scratch every time a new broker calls.
