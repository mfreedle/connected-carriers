What the driver sees

No app. No login. Works on any phone.

The driver receives a single SMS with a link. They tap it when they arrive. One button confirms their location. The whole interaction takes under 10 seconds.

The geofence is invisible to the driver. The distance check happens behind the scenes — they never see the radius or the result.

Why it matters

A phone confirms from miles away. The timing doesn't match. The load moves before anyone notices.

This gives you a signal before that happens. A timestamped, location-based record that the driver was — or wasn't — where they said they'd be.

It won't catch everything. But it gives you more to work with than a phone call and a promise.
