About Connected Carriers

Connected Carriers helps freight brokers qualify carriers faster and clear the right truck before the load moves.

Connected Carriers is a broker-side carrier qualification and dispatch readiness platform built for the realities of freight operations.

Freight brokers lose time and take on risk when carrier setup is fragmented across email, phone calls, spreadsheets, compliance tools, and load boards. Connected Carriers brings that workflow into one place.

With Connected Carriers, brokers can:

  • collect carrier interest and setup information
  • request required compliance documents
  • review and verify setup packets
  • track certificate expiration dates
  • manage dispatch packets for specific loads
  • confirm that required information is in place before clearing a truck to roll

The platform is designed to support the real workflow brokers already live in, not force them into a generic software process.

Connected Carriers is a HoneXAI product.

We're building Connected Carriers to reduce friction for good carriers and reduce risk for brokers who need to move quickly without cutting corners.
