About

Built for the freight community.

Connected Carriers is a carrier qualification and dispatch clearance platform for freight brokers. It exists to solve one specific problem: the manual, repetitive, and error-prone work of vetting carriers, collecting compliance documents, and verifying dispatch details before every load.

Every broker has a version of this problem. Carriers submit the same paperwork to twenty different brokers. Brokers chase down the same COIs, W-9s, and carrier agreements over and over. And at dispatch, insurance lapses, VIN mismatches, and missing rate confirmations still happen — because there's no system forcing every check to happen before the truck rolls.

Connected Carriers is that system.

Who it serves

Freight brokers who want a faster, safer way to onboard carriers and clear loads for dispatch — without rebuilding their entire operation.

Carriers who are tired of filling out a new packet for every broker they want to work with.

Who built it

Connected Carriers is a product of HoneXAI — a technology company focused on building practical operational tools for industries that move physical goods.

Current status

Connected Carriers is in active development and early pilot use. The platform is not yet open to the general public. Broker and carrier interest can be submitted through the main site.
