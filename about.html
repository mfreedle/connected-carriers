About Connected Carriers

Connected Carriers helps freight brokers filter inbound carriers, automate the doc chase, and know if something changed at pickup — before the load is released.

Between booking a carrier and the load actually moving, brokers lose hours to manual qualification calls, chasing dispatch for missing documents, and hoping nothing changes at the dock. Connected Carriers closes that gap.

What we do

Connected Carriers is a lightweight broker workflow layer with three functions:

Filter. When carriers respond to your load posting, the system checks their MC against FMCSA instantly — authority, safety rating, and basic context. You see who's qualified before you pick up the phone.

Chase. When you assign a carrier, the system sends a doc request and follows up automatically. Two reminders, ten minutes apart. If they go quiet, you know. If they respond, you know.

Signal. When the driver arrives at pickup, you get a confirmation based on time and location. If something doesn't line up, you get a warning before the load moves.

How it works

Connected Carriers sits on top of your existing workflow. There's no TMS to replace, no app for drivers to download, and no system for carriers to learn. Everything runs through simple links and texts.

Every carrier who comes through the system builds a profile over time. The more loads you run, the fewer docs you chase — because carriers who complete their profile once are dispatch-ready the next time.
